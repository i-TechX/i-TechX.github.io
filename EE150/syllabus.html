二、课程简介和教学目的

Introduction: This course covers important methods of modern signal and system analysis, especially the linear time-invariant systems and Fourier analysis.
                
Objectives: Understand the concepts and techniques, and apply them in new systems.

三、教学内容、教学方式和学时安排

Course contents: Linear time-invariant systems, Fourier series, continuous-time Fourier transform, discrete-time Fourier transform, time and frequeny characterization, Laplace transform, and Z transform. 

Teaching: Class teaching.
