(a) How many words of length 5 are there?

We impose now the following two rules: the same letter can not appear consecutively (eg: the word "faaec" is not allowed because a appears twice consecutively), and two consonants can not appear consecutively (eg: "efcae" is not allowed)

(b) How many words of length 5 starting with f and ending with e are there?

(c) How many words of length 5 starting with f are there?

(d) How many words of length 5 are there?

(e) How many of those words of length 5 have no e?
