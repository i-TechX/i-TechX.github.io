Problem 6
?/? point (graded)

Alice and Bob are playing a game with the following rules: there are 2 white balls and 3 black balls. The players play in turn. At each turn they can withdraw one ball or two balls with different colors. The winner is the one who withdraw the last ball(s) (i.e. a winning position is (1,0), (0,1), or (1,1), where the pair (x,y) means that there are still x white balls and y black balls on the table).

(a) Draw the corresponding game tree.

(b) Assign values to the vertices.

(c) Assume Alice is the first player. Who wins the game if Alice and Bob follow an optimal strategy?
